About Bizhub

Bizhub is a versatile platform, engaging in a variety of projects from international trade to investment immigration through the EB-5 Program. Our startup incubator focuses on nurturing emerging businesses. In our Multi-Cultural Town, we celebrate the richness of diverse cultures, while "The Flow" in Orlando showcases the charm of modern gastronomy. Additionally, we collaborate with the Asian American Hospitality Alliance and the American Culinary Federation (ACF) to foster growth and innovation in hospitality and culinary arts. Bizhub is where opportunities and cultures converge, creating a dynamic environment for growth and creativity.
